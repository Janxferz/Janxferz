Who is LogHead?

LogHead is an alter ego of Janxferz. Originally having his name show up in the album cover of Janxferz's album, "FuFu Fuckface," LogHead is Janxferz from another dimension. He was originally sent to Janxferz's dimension to get info on what other dimensions are like. However, after losing contact with his team, LogHead was now stuck in Janxferz's realm, and had to live life there for a while until he could get back to his dimension. LogHead has said previously that he met Janxferz at a Speedway Gas Station. As the story goes, LogHead was working the night shift, until at 3:00 a.m. when Janxferz came in. Looking for a Faygo, Janxferz asked LogHead if they had any, to which LogHead ended up starting a conversation with Janxferz about how they both enjoyed Faygo. This conversation ended up with Janxferz asking LogHead for his contact, and about a month later, Janxferz contacted LogHead, asking about a collaboration.

Why so much into Vaporwave?

To be honest, I'm not entirely sure why I'm into Vaporwave so much. Maybe it's the bright and sort of pastel colors, maybe it's the calming and retro music that gives me a weird feeling of nostalgia, maybe it's the art with all of the busts and statues. Whatever it is, I'm really into it for some reason that I can't quite place my finger on.
